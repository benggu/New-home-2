Deltarune Media Recommendations

Section 1: Written Works

fancomics, fanfiction and such

  • Togore Time

    • Official Site/ Artist's Tumblr/ Artist's Twitter

    Y'know that one popular fanmade character everyone went crazy over that one time? Togore? This webcomic is centered around them. The comic itself feels very grounded, yet heartwarming, and there's a lot of character exploration even though the main character's a fanmade one. Very much enjoyed.

  • Twin Runes

    • Artist's Tumblr(hosted here)

    What if there was a portal to Deltarune in Undertale? Fun AU about that exact scenario! I'll admit I haven't quite kept up with this one yet, but I liked what I've read so far(~page 52). Also fascinated by how the artist draws Kris... They shall forever have braces in my heart from now on.

  • the nice dreemurr boy

    • link(ao3)

    (don't think I can do this one justice, so I'll just copy the summary)

    Sometimes, she thought about what might have happened if things had gone the way the adults imagined. She’d marry Kris. Probably stay in town. She’d go to church with her mom. Kris would wear a nice shirt and stand quietly beside her. Maybe they’d live in a quiet house near the pond where the ducks still came in spring. It wouldn’t be bad. It might even be nice.
    —on growing up together and the expectations placed on you because of it

  • HoliPlays AU

    • link(ao3)

    React fic! Rudy and Noelle play Deltarune. Came across this one on Reddit haha. It's sadly discontinued, but it was entertaining all throughout.

Section 2: Video Works

  • NORTHERNBLIGHT_225
    • link(youtube)

    What if Noelle acted like a creepypasta anomaly? Not all that scary really, leans more towards black comedy/shitposting a lot of the time. this video in particular (of a kid coming across Noelle) still cracks me up, eerie as it is. Ongoing!

  • z Ke
    • link(youtube)

    They make a lot of different animations, but it seems they're on a deltarune kick as of late. Mostly animations of boss battles. Impressive-ass animations, mind you, they have very very high quality and it's unbelievable how fast they're coming out (every couple months?!).
