About the House of Housepen

The website House of Housepen was created by Benggu on the 24th of April, 2024.

In it's current iteration, Housepen exists to bring users a feeling of fresh interactivity and community within the projects and sub-pages it hosts. It's design(in both visuals and philosophy) was inspired by (the late) Korean children's portal site Junior Naver.
